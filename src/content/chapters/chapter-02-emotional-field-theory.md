Chapter 2 — Emotional Field Theory 

How Feeling Becomes Gravity 

Dream reality does not respond to force or mass. 

It responds to feeling. 

If Chapter 1 established what exists in the Dream Universe, this chapter explains what makes it 
move. 

1. The Prime Postulate of Dream Dynamics 

All motion, transformation, and causation in dream-space originate in emotional change. 

This is the foundation: 

Emotion is the fundamental force of dream physics. 

Where gravity curves space in waking physics, emotion curves perceptual reality in dreams. 

We call this governing principle: 

The Law of Emotional Causality 

Definition: 

Changes in emotional intensity produce proportional distortions in scenery, 
identity, time flow, and narrative direction. 

Nothing moves in a dream because “something hit it.” 

Things move because you felt something. 

2. The Emotional Gravity Model 

Each emotion projects an Affective Gravity Field that bends dream environments toward states 
consistent with the emotion. 

We define: 

Emotional Valence (V) 

Positive ↔ negative charge of feeling 

(love, curiosity vs fear, grief) 

Emotional Intensity (I) 

Magnitude or strength of feeling 

Emotional Coherence (C) 

How focused or scattered the emotional state is 

Together they determine dream dynamics via the core field equation: 

Dream Distortion ∝ V × I / C 

Interpretation: 

-

Intense, focused emotions warp dream reality dramatically. 

-  Weak or scattered emotions produce gentle, diffuse landscapes.

-  Highly incoherent emotion fragments space completely.

Emotional Singularity Point

5. Scene Collapse Dynamics 

Dream scenes persist only while emotional coherence remains stable. 

When emotional state shifts abruptly: 

Scene Collapse occurs: 

• Environments dissolve 

• Rooms fold into new architectures 

• Characters morph into unrelated figures 

• Time jumps 

Scene collapse is not random. 

It follows the rule: 

A scene persists only while emotional narrative alignment remains coherent. 

We model this by: 

Scene Lifetime ∝ Emotional Consistency

Not transitions between scenes. 

It transitions between emotional permissions.

7. Emotional Propulsion Mechanics 

Dream locomotion obeys emotional drive rather than muscular force. 

Emotion → Motion Conversion
